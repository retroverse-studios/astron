Sun through Pluto, lunar nodes, Chiron, Lilith — retrogrades flagged, seven house systems, applying and separating aspects.

Tropical and sidereal side by side, with Lahiri, Raman, Krishnamurti and Fagan-Bradley ayanamsas. Full varga set included.

> TRANSITS & RETURNS

Any moment's sky against your chart. Solar and lunar returns, secondary progressions, solar arc directions, dashas.

Inter-aspects, house overlays, composite and Davison charts — and a compatibility bouquet instead of a fake percentage.

> REAL EPHEMERIS

The Swiss Ephemeris compiled to WebAssembly. Professional-grade planetary positions, computed entirely client-side.

> FULLY PRIVATE

No server, no accounts, no analytics. Your birth data never leaves your machine — in the browser or on the desktop.

> WHAT THIS IS

ASTRON is a real astronomical calculation engine attached to an old interpretive tradition. The positions are as accurate as professional software — it is the same ephemeris. Whether they mean anything about your life is a question for the tradition, not for the maths, and we keep the two clearly separated.

The maths is real and shown honestly. The meaning belongs to the tradition, not to fate.
